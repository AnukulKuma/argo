Why Amoli International

We focus on product categories that are directly connected to the daily lives of many people in our society. Therefore we, as Amoli International take a participating position towards sustainability themes that are most relevant in our field of operations of rice export from India and other agri commodity. Our main focus is on the commitment of delivering against appropriate safety and quality standards. Due to the nature of our operations we also are aware that our direct impact on the environment is limited to our business operations. By understanding our key drivers for sustainability, we can work towards integrating relevant sustainability measures in our overall business and operating strategy.

Due to our central role in the respective supply chain of the products as FMCG products, organic spices or grains that we deliver, we understand the needs and wants of our clients both upstream and downstream. At one side we can translate the requirements of buyers into clear sourcing strategies and at the other side we are also able to explain relevant matters that emerge from the supply side.

At operations level we aim towards working in a way that we are aware of the effects on the environment that emerges from any of activities as rice export or edible oil export. In this regard we understand that our direct visible impact on the environment results from our office activities, commuting and business travel.
